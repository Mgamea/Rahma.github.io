About Rahma Islamic Centre

Rahma Islamic Centre is a vibrant community center dedicated to serving Muslims and fostering understanding among all people. We provide a welcoming space for prayer, education, and community engagement.

Our center offers daily prayers, Islamic education programs, youth activities, and community events that bring people together in the spirit of unity and compassion.

Join us as we work together to build a stronger, more connected community grounded in faith and mutual respect.

Our Activities & Services

Daily Five Prayers

Join us for all five daily prayers in congregation. Our prayer hall is open throughout the day for individual prayers as well.

Kids Quran Class

Engaging Quranic education for children of all ages. We teach proper recitation, memorization, and understanding.

Community Events

Regular community gatherings, iftars, Eid celebrations, and social events that strengthen our bonds.

Islamic Library

Access a comprehensive collection of Islamic books, resources, and educational materials for all ages.

Youth Programs

Dynamic programs designed for young Muslims including sports, mentorship, and leadership development.

Marriage Services

Complete Islamic marriage services including nikah ceremonies, counseling, and family support programs.

Our Mission

At Rahma Islamic Centre, our mission is to provide a comprehensive Islamic environment that nurtures spiritual growth, education, and community development.

We strive to:

  • Facilitate regular Islamic worship and prayers
  • Provide quality Islamic education for all ages
  • Build bridges of understanding with the wider community
  • Support families through counseling and programs
  • Promote charity and social welfare initiatives
  • Create a welcoming space for all Muslims
