About Us

The Winnipeg Rahma Islamic Centre is a vibrant and inclusive Islamic Center dedicated to serving the spiritual, educational, and social needs of Muslims in Winnipeg. Our centre is a welcoming space for individuals of all ages, background, or level of religious knowledge, providing an environment where faith, learning, and community come together. Our mosque is committed to the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). We strive to promote compassion, and unity among our members and the broader community. With a focus on both religious education and community development, we offer various services to enrich the lives of our members and foster a spirit of brotherhood and sisterhood.

Mission Statement

Our mission at the Winnipeg Rahma Islamic Centre is to create an environment of spiritual growth, education, and social welfare that nurtures individuals and strengthens the community. We aim to inspire people of all ages to live a life guided by Islamic principles, to engage in continuous learning, and to promote harmony and understanding within the broader society. We seek to:

  • Foster a sense of belonging and support within the Muslim community
  • Promote the values of compassion, justice, and service as guided by the Quran and the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him)
  • Offer educational programs that nurture both faith and intellect
  • Engage in charitable and social services to support those in need

Our Values

At the Winnipeg Rahma Islamic Centre, we are guided by the following core values:

Faith

We prioritize nurturing a deep connection with Allah (SWT) through worship, prayer, and reflection on the teachings of Islam.

Community

We believe in fostering a sense of brotherhood and sisterhood, where all members feel welcome, supported, and included.

Education

We are committed to lifelong learning, offering programs that enhance understanding of the Quran, Hadith, and Islamic teachings.

Service

We believe in giving back to the community through charitable acts, support for the needy, and initiatives that benefit the broader society.

Integrity

We strive to uphold the highest moral and ethical standards in all our dealings, reflecting the principles of honesty, fairness, and respect.

Compassion

We encourage kindness and care for others, whether within our Muslim community or beyond, as we seek to follow the example of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).
