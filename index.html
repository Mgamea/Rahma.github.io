About Rahma Islamic Centre

Winnipeg Rahma Islamic Centre is a religious organization that seeks to meet the spiritual needs of Muslims in Winnipeg, and we are located in the Kildonan area. Rahma was founded in 2016 to support our community to engage in their religion and to provide spiritual services and a safe space for religious activities.

Our Activities & Services

Sisters' Halaqa

A dedicated Quran class for women, focusing on studying the Quran, exploring its meanings, and sharing spiritual reflections in a welcoming environment.

Daily Five Prayers

The masjid is open every day for all five prayers, providing a peaceful and welcoming environment for worshippers. Friday prayer (Jumu'ah) takes place at 12:50 p.m. at Chalmers Community Centre.

Kids Quran Class

A Quran class for all ages is held every weekend, focusing on recitation, understanding, and memorization of the Holy Quran.

Study Club

The After School Study Club at the masjid offers students a supportive environment to complete homework, study, and strengthen their academic skills.

Quran Hifz

We offer a structured Quran memorization class for those dedicated to learning and preserving the Quran in their hearts.

Our Mission

Our mission at the Winnipeg Rahma Islamic Centre is to create an environment of spiritual growth, education, and social welfare that nurtures individuals and strengthens the community. We aim to inspire people of all ages to live a life guided by Islamic principles, to engage in continuous learning, and to promote harmony and understanding within the broader society.
