Activities & Services

Our Activities & Services

Daily Five Prayers

Join us for all five daily prayers in congregation. Our prayer hall is open throughout the day for individual prayers as well.

Kids Quran Class

Engaging Quranic education for children of all ages. We teach proper recitation, memorization, and understanding.

Community Events

Regular community gatherings, iftars, Eid celebrations, and social events that strengthen our bonds.

Islamic Library

Access a comprehensive collection of Islamic books, resources, and educational materials for all ages.

Youth Programs

Dynamic programs designed for young Muslims including sports, mentorship, and leadership development.

Marriage Services

Complete Islamic marriage services including nikah ceremonies, counseling, and family support programs.
